About

27 Dimensions is a project by independent artist and producer, John Hitchcock. With inperation from early 2000s Alternative and Post-Grunge.


Formed in 2025 and released the debut single "Flowers" on March 27 2026, 27 Dimensions has stood up to modern music in an attempt to bring back the 2000s Alternative scene.
